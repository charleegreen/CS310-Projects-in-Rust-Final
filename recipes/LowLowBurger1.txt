Crab Cake Burgers
Total Time = 15 Minutes
Total Calories = 175 per serving
6 Servings

INGREDIENTS

1 pound crabmeat
1 egg, lightly beaten
1/2 cup panko breadcrumbs, (see Note)
¼ cup light mayonnaise
2 tablespoons minced chives
1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
1 tablespoon lemon juice
1 teaspoon celery seed
1 teaspoon onion powder
¼ teaspoon freshly ground pepper
4 dashes of hot sauce, such as
1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil
2 teaspoons unsalted butter


DIRECTIONS

1. Mix crab, egg, breadcrumbs, mayonnaise, chives, mustard, lemon juice, celery seed, onion powder, pepper, and hot sauce in a large bowl. 
Form into 6 patties.

2. Heat oil and butter in a large nonstick skillet over medium heat until the butter stops foaming. Cook the patties until golden brown, 
about 4 minutes per side.
