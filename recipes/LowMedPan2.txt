Oatmeal Pancakes
Total time = 20 minutes
Total Calories per 2 pancakes = 263 calories

INGREDIENTS

1/2 cup plus 2 tablespoons quick-cooking oats
1/2 cup whole wheat flour
1/2 cup all-purpose flour
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/3 cup packed brown sugar
1 large egg
2 tablespoons canola oil


DIRECTIONS

1. In a small bowl, combine the oats, flour, baking soda, salt, and brown sugar. In another small bowl, beat the egg, oil and buttermilk.
 Stir into dry ingredients just until moistened.
2. Pour batter by 1/3 cupfuls onto a greased large cast-iron skillet or griddle over medium heat. Turn when bubbles form on top; cook until
 the second side is golden brown.
