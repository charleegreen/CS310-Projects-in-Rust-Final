Chinese Style Vermicelli (Rice Noodles) Recipe
Total Time= 15 min
Calories= 271 per serving
4 servings

INGREDIENTS

1 (8 ounce) package dried rice noodles
2 tablespoons vegetable oil
1 clove garlic, minced
1 tablespoon soy sauce
½ tablespoon chili sauce
salt and pepper to taste
1 green onion, chopped

DIRECTIONS

1. Fill a large pot with water and bring to a rolling boil; stir in rice noodles and return to a boil. Cook until al dente, 2 to 3 minutes; 
try not to overcook as they will become mushy. Drain.

2. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add garlic and sauté until tender. Stir in noodles; season with soy sauce, chili sauce, 
salt, and pepper. Sprinkle with chopped green onion.
