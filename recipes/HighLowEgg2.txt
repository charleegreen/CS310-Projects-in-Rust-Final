Eggs with Avocado and Salsa Recipe
Total Time=20 min
Calories= 505 per serving
2 servings

INGREDIENTS

Eggs 4 large (200g)
Avocados, diced 2 (402g)
Salsa 6 tbsp (108g)

DIRECTIONS 

1. Cook eggs according to your preference (scrambled, sunny side up, etc.)
2. Season with some salt and pepper and top with the diced avocado and salsa.
3. Serve.
