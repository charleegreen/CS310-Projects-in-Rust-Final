Spicy Chicken Breasts Recipe
Total TIme= 30 min
Calories= 173 per serving
4 servings

INGREDIENTS

2 ½ tablespoons paprika
2 tablespoons garlic powder
1 tablespoon salt
1 tablespoon onion powder
1 tablespoon dried thyme
1 tablespoon ground cayenne pepper
1 tablespoon ground black pepper
4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves

DIRECTIONS

1. Preheat the grill for medium-high heat. Lightly oil the grill grate.

2. Mix together paprika, garlic powder, salt, onion powder, thyme, cayenne pepper, and ground black pepper in a bowl. Set aside 3 
tablespoons spice mixture; store remainder in an airtight container for later use.

3. Rub reserved 3 tablespoons spice mixture all over chicken breasts.

4. Cook chicken on the preheated grill until juices run clear, 6 to 8 minutes on each side. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the 
center should read at least 165 degrees F (74 degrees C).
