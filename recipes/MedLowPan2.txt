Medium Carb Waffles
Total time = 15 minutes
Total Calories= 281

INGREDIENTS

⅛ Cup Almond Flour
½ Scoop Protein Powder
1 Tablespoon Sweetener - Your Choice
½ Teaspoon Baking Powder
1 Large Whole Egg
2 Large Egg Whites
1 Teaspoon Vanilla Extract
2 Tablespoons Greek Yogurt

DIRECTIONS

1. Take out a bowl, combine all of your ingredients in it, and mix everything together. 
2. Let your waffle maker heat up, coat it with some non-stick cooking spray, and pour your mix in.1w
3. Wait and repeat!
