Protein Banana Nut Waffles
Total Time = 17 Miuntes
Total Calories = 399

INGREDIENTS

1 ½ Cups Rolled Oats
2 Scoops Protein Powder - Vanilla
2 ½ Teaspoons Baking Powder
½ Teaspoon Salt
4 Teaspoons Sweetener
1 ½ Teaspoons Ground Cinnamon
1 Banana
1 Teaspoon Vanilla Extract
1 Large Whole Egg
4 Ounces Milk Substitute - or Milk
½ Cup Cottage Cheese - Fat Free (or Greek Yogurt)
¼ Cup Walnuts - Chopped (Or whatever type nuts you prefer)

DIRECTIONS

1. Add your Rolled Oats into either a food processor or blender
2. Process/blend them until they look like flour
3. Add all of your dry ingredients into either your food processor or a large bowl
4. Process/mix your dry ingredients
5. Add in your wet ingredients
6. Mix those in
7. Turn your waffle maker on and coat it with non-stick cooking spray
8. Pour your mix in and cook for however long your waffler maker says to
