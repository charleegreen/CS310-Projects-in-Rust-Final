Smoked Salmon and Broccoli Frittata
Total Time=15 min
Calories=165 per serving
2 Servings

INGREDIENTS

100g tenderstem broccoli, trimmed
1 tsp olive oil
3 large British Lion eggs
salt and freshly ground black pepper
25g smoked salmon, chopped
1 tbsp chopped fresh dill (or 1 tsp freeze dried)

DIRECTIONS

1. Cook the broccoli in boiling water for 3 mins or until just tender. Drain in a colander and rinse in cold water until cold. Drain.
2. Heat the oil in a medium non-stick frying pan. Beat the eggs with seasoning. Add the broccoli to the pan and sauté for 2 mins. Pour over 
the eggs and cook for 3-4 mins or until the base has set.
3. Scatter the salmon and dill over the frittata and cook under a hot grill for 2 mins or until the top has set. Cut into wedges and serve 
with salad.
