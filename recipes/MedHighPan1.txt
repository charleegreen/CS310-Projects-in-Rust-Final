No Knead Mediterranean Bread
Total Time = 50 minutes
Total Calories = 275

INGREDIENTS

3 cups wheat flour
1½ cup warm water
½ tsp yeast
½ tsp salt
5 sun-dried tomatoes
3-4 Mediterranean olives

DIRECTIONS

1. Combining the flour, yeast first before adding the salt in a large bowl
2. Add warm water to activate the yeast. Combine the water and flour with a wooden spoon or with your hand. When the flour is absorbed by 
the water you are done
3. Take cling film and cover the bowl. Let it rise overnight
4. Pre heat your Dutch oven when you heat your oven to 500℉/250℃
5. Take the dough out of the bowl on a lightly floured surface. Chop the sun dried tomatoes and olives. Fold them in the dough top to bottom 
and left to right into a ball
6. Take the Dutch oven out of the oven and put the dough on parchment paper in the pan, fold side up
7. Put the lid on the Dutch oven and put the pan in the oven for 30 minutes
8. Then take the lid of the pan and bake the bread for another 15 minutes to get a crusty no knead bread
9. Cool on a rack and your bread is ready
