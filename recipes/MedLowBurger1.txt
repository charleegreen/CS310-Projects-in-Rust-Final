Honey Mustard Turkey Burger
Total Time = 20 minutes
Total Calories per Serving = 317
4 Servings

INGREDIENTS

¼ cup coarse-grained mustard
2 tablespoons honey
1 pound ground turkey breast
¼ teaspoon salt
¼ teaspoon freshly ground pepper
2 teaspoons canola oil
4 whole-wheat hamburger rolls, split and toasted
Lettuce, tomato slices, and red onion slices, for garnish


DIRECTIONS

1. Prepare a grill.

2. Whisk mustard and honey in a small bowl until smooth.

3. Combine turkey, 3 tablespoons of the mustard mixture, salt and pepper in a bowl; mix well. Form into four 1-inch-thick burgers.

4. Lightly brush the burgers on both sides with oil. Grill until no pink remains in center, 5 to 7 minutes per side. Brush the burgers with 
the remaining mustard mixture. Serve on rolls with lettuce, tomato and onion slices.
