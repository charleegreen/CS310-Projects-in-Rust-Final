Orange Coconut Protein Smoothie Recipe
Calories= 770

This orange coconut smoothie is another great alternative to the traditional fruit smoothie. It’s slightly sweet and tart with a nutty
 flavor as well.

Use fresh-squeezed orange juice, if you can. It will have the best flavor.


INGREDIENTS

1 cup orange juice
1/2 cup coconut milk
3 tbsp vanilla protein powder
1 banana
1/2 avocado
1/2 cup ice
1/2 tsp orange zest
