Pancake Tacos
Total Time= 30 Minutes
Calories= 200 Calories

INGREDIENTS

1 cup Krusteaz Protein Buttermilk Pancake Mix
1 cup milk
1 large egg
1 teaspoon Taylor & Colledge vanilla bean paste
2 to 3 tablespoons butter, melted
4 large bananas, sliced
2 tablespoons butter
4 tablespoons Torani caramel sauce plus more for garnish


DIRECTIONS

1. Combine the pancake mix with the milk, egg, and vanilla bean paste until just combined.
2. Heat a skillet over medium heat. Brush some butter in the pan before pouring 1/4 cup of the batter into the pan.
3. Cook until bubbling on top and golden brown underneath. Turn the pancakes over and cook until lightly browned on the other side.
4. Continue cooking the pancakes until you have used all the batter.
5. Return the heat to the pan.
6. Add 2 tablespoons of butter to the pan. Once the butter is melted and bubbled, add the bananas.
7. Saute until soft. Stir in 2 tablespoons caramel sauce and saute a few minutes more.
8. Place the pancakes in a taco holder. Top with the bananas and an additional drizzle of caramel syrup and serve.
