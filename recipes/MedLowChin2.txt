Chinese Chicken Sald Recipe
Total Time= 15 min
Calories= 361 per serving
6 servings

INGREDIENTS

1 rotisserie chicken, meat removed and shredded
½ medium head cabbage, chopped
4 green onions, sliced
1 (3 ounce) package ramen noodles (such as Nissin® Top Ramen)
3 tablespoons sesame seeds
3 tablespoons slivered almonds

Dressing:
½ cup olive oil
3 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
2 tablespoons white sugar
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon ground black pepper

DIRECTIONS

Toss chicken meat, cabbage, onions, ramen noodles, sesame seeds, and almonds together in a large bowl. Beat olive oil, vinegar, sugar, salt, 
and pepper together in a small bowl; drizzle over the salad and toss to coat.
