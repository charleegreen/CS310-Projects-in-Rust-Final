Protein French Toast Sticks
Total Time = 20 minutes
Total Calories = 304 Calories

INGREDIENTS

6 slices brioche bread
2 large eggs
1/3 cup egg whites (80g)
1/4 cup almond milk (60g)
1/2 cup vanilla protein powder (62)
1 tsp vanilla extract
1 tsp liquid stevia
1 tsp cinnamon


DIRECTIONS

1. Cut the bread into 3-4 slices (depending on how wide your bread is). About an inch thick.
2. Whisk together all the remaining ingredients in a bowl. 
3. Then dip each stick into the mixture and set on a plate.
4. Heat a skillet over medium heat. Spray with cooking spray and add the french toast sticks in a single layer, not touching. Cook for 2-3 
minutes on each side or until browned to a liking.
5. To assemble, divide the french toast sticks among four plates (or containers) and sprinkle with powdered sugar.    
6. Serve with fresh berries, sausage patties, and a cup of maple syrup for dipping.
