Zucchini Smoothie Recipe
Calories= 118

This zucchini smoothie is a blend of orange juice, banana, and fresh zucchini that makes a great-tasting breakfast. And don't worry — you 
can't even taste the zucchini in it!

INGREDIENTS

½ cup ice cubes, or as needed (Optional)
½ zucchini, shredded
½ frozen banana
½ cup orange juice
