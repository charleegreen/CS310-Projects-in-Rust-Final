Chocolate Chip Peanut Butter Muffins
Total Time = 25 minutes
Total Calories = 137

INGREDIENTS

1 cup almond Flour
1/2 cup allulose
1 tsp baking powder
1 pinch salt
1/3 cup Peanut Butter natural
1/3 cup almond milk
2 eggs
1/2 cup Lilly's Dark Chocolate Chips

INSTRUCTIONS

1. Preheat oven to 350°F
2. Combine all the dry ingredients (except chocolate chips) in a large mixing bowl and stir.
3. Add in the peanut butter and almond milk and mix thoroughly
4. Add eggs and again mix thoroughly
5. Fold in chocolate chips.
6. put muffin liners in a 12 well muffin tin. (you could also grease the muffin tin instead of using muffin liners if you are brave)
7. Bake for about 15 minutes at 350°F.
