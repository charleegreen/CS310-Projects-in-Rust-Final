Baked Fish Fillet Recipe
Total Time= 35 min
Calories= 165 per serving
3 servings

INGREDIENTS

1 pound flounder fillets
½ teaspoon salt
ground black pepper to taste
1 tablespoon lemon juice
2 teaspoons melted butter
1 teaspoon minced onion

DIRECTIONS

1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).

2. Place flounder in a baking dish; season with salt and pepper. Mix together lemon juice, melted butter, and onion in a small bowl; pour 
over flounder.

3. Bake in the preheated oven until fish is opaque and flakes easily with a fork, 25 to 30 minutes.
