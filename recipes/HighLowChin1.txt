Crunchy Chinese Pork Salad Recipe
Total Time=20 min
Calories=570 

INGREDIENTS

3/4 lb. roasted pork loin (cubed)
4 slices bacon (diced)
6 cups iceberg lettuce (torn)
8 oz. water chestnuts (sliced, drained)
1/2 cup green onions (sliced)
3 Tbsp. soy sauce
2 Tbsp. honey
1 Tbsp. ketchup
1 tsp. dry mustard
3 oz. chow mein noodles

DIRECTIONS

In a large skillet, cook bacon just until crisp; drain and set aside. In a large bowl toss together bacon, pork, lettuce, water chestnuts
 and green onions. For dressing, in small bowl stir together soy sauce, honey, catsup and dry mustard. Add dressing and chow mein noodles to
 salad; tossing lightly to coat.
