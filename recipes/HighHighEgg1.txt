Cheese and Tomato Quiche Recipe
Total Time= 70 min
Calories= 573 per serving
6 servings

INGREDIENTS

2 cups cheddar cheese (grated)
12 large eggs
1/2 cup cream cheese
12 tbsp butter (melted)
1 medium or 2 small tomatoes (chopped)
1/4 cup onion (chopped)
1/8 tsp thyme
1/4 tsp dried parsley
salt & black pepper (to taste)

DIRECTIONS

1. Add 1.5 cups of cheddar cheese to a baking dish (8" x 12").
2. Melt butter in a microwave.
3. Now add eggs, cream cheese, tomatoes, and onion to the food processor. Add in thyme, dried parsley, and salt with pepper.  If you don’t 
own a food processor, whisk eggs in a bowl with cream cheese and mix in tomatoes with onions with the rest of the ingredients.
4. While processing the ingredients, slowly start pouring in melted butter. In the case of no processor, just pour in the butter while 
mixing all the ingredients together.
5. Now, pour the egg mixture over the shredded cheese into a baking dish.
6. Sprinkle with the remaining ½ cups of cheese.
7. Bake in the oven at 325F for 45 minutes. The outside should be completely cooked, while the middle only a little jiggly.
8. Cooldown before serving! The recipe is freezer-friendly!
