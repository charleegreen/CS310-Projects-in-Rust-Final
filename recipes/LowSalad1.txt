Salad of Avocado and romaine with Black Olive Dressing
Total time = 20 Minutes
Total Calories per Serving = 192
4 Servings

INGREDIENTS

3 tablespoons orange juice
2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
¼ cup pitted Kalamata or other brine-cured black olives, coarsely chopped
1 clove garlic, finely chopped
1 teaspoon granulated sugar
¼ teaspoon salt
Ground pepper to taste
1 firm ripe avocado, thinly sliced
½ small red onion, very thinly sliced
½ head romaine lettuce, thinly sliced


DIRECTIONS

Whisk orange juice, oil, olives, garlic and sugar in a medium bowl. Season with salt and pepper. Add avocado and onion; toss lightly to 
coat.

Spread romaine on a serving platter. Top with the avocado mixture.
