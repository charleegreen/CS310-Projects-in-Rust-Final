7 Layer Dutch Oven Country Breakfast Recipe
Total Time= 60 min
Calories= 869 per serving
6 servings

INGREDIENTS

32 oz Ground Sausage - 2 small packages
30 oz Frozen Shredded potatoes
12 eggs
2 Cups shredded cheddar cheese
1 can biscuit dough or homemade biscuit mix
salt and pepper to taste

DIRECTIONS

1. While the coals are heating up place the dutch oven over then and cook the sausage until browned. 
2. Remove from heat and pour the package of hashbrowns over it. 
3. Then crack the 12 eggs over the potato layer as evenly as possible.
4. Sprinkle the cheddar cheese as the next layer.
5. Then place the biscuits all over the cheese or homemade biscuit mix.
6. Cover the dutch oven and then add coals on the top and bottom of dutch oven.
7. Allow to cook for 45 minutes. Serve.
